What's the rating scale?

This affects how bar graphs and rankings work throughout the whole app

A

1–10

Classic. Lots of granularity. Works well for bar graphs showing score distribution. IMDB uses this.

B

1–5 Stars

Simple and familiar. Easy to rate quickly. Less nuance but lower friction.

C

1–100

Maximum granularity. Great for ranked lists (your #1 show scored 94, #2 scored 91). Letterboxd uses a 0.5–5 variant.

D

Custom / Flexible

Different scales per media type (e.g. stars for movies, 1–10 for games). More complex to implement but flexible.
